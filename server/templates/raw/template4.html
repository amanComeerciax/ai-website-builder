About

"I make things beautiful, and I make beautiful things work."

Based in Berlin, I've spent the last 8 years helping founders, startups, and established brands find their visual voice. I believe great design is invisible — it just feels right.

Before going independent, I led design at AKQA Berlin and Publicis. Now I work directly with clients who want something meaningful, not just something made.

FAQ

Common Questions

How does the process work? +
We start with a 1-hour discovery call, followed by a proposal and scope. Once approved, I work in two-week sprints with regular check-ins.
Do you work with startups? +
Yes — some of my best work is with early-stage companies. Getting the identity right from the start makes everything easier later.
What files will I receive? +
All source files (Figma, Illustrator, or Photoshop), print-ready PDFs, web-optimized PNGs/SVGs, and a brand guidelines document.
Do you offer retainers? +
Yes. Monthly creative retainers start at €1,200/month for 10 hours of ongoing design work. Ideal for teams that need consistent support.
