Our Philosophy

Fewer things. Better things.

VESPER was founded on the belief that a thoughtful wardrobe requires fewer, better pieces — not more. We work with mills in Portugal and Japan to source fabrics that age beautifully and wear honestly.

Every garment is designed in London and made in small batches. No excess stock. No landfill. No compromises.

Our Values

Sustainably Sourced

FAQ

Common Questions

What is your return policy? +
Free returns within 30 days of delivery. Items must be unworn with tags attached. We process refunds within 5 business days.
How do I know my size? +
Every product page includes a detailed size guide and fit notes. When in doubt, email us and our stylists will help you choose.
Where are your clothes made? +
All our garments are made in Portugal in audited, transparent factories. We visit our manufacturing partners twice a year.
Do you offer alterations? +
Yes! Insider members receive free lifetime alterations and repairs. Non-members can access alterations at cost plus postage.
