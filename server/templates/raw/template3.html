Our Story

"Food is an act of love, executed with fire."

Founded by Chef Alejandro Reyes, Fuego was born from a vision: to bring the true spirit of Mexican cuisine to a modern table. Not Tex-Mex. Not fusion. Something deeper.

Every recipe begins with memory — grandmother's mole, uncle's tacos al pastor, market spices from Oaxaca. We honor those roots, then push them forward.

FAQ

Good questions deserve good answers

Do you take walk-ins?
+
We love spontaneity! Walk-ins are welcome at the bar and some tables nightly. For the dining room, reservations are recommended, especially on weekends.
Is there a vegetarian menu?
+
Absolutely. Our seasonal vegetarian menu features dishes like rajas con crema, huitlacoche quesadillas, and more. Ask your server for options.
What is the dress code?
+
Smart casual. We want you to feel comfortable and look great. The bar area is more relaxed; the dining room is elegant but not formal.
Can I bring my own wine?
+
We have an extensive wine and mezcal list, but you're welcome to bring a special bottle. A corkage fee of $35 applies.
