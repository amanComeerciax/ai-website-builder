Unyielding Standards.

We do not blend. We do not compromise. We seek out the anomalies—the high-altitude mutations and obsessive fermentations that produce cups of startling clarity.

Our roasting process is entirely manual, relying on sight, sound, and smell. Software cannot taste the moment sugar caramelizes into complexity before collapsing into ash.

The Method

Isolation

Selecting singular lots separated by day of harvest rather than aggregated into regional blends.

Restraint

Applying heat with absolute caution to develop sweetness without masking intrinsic floral notes.

Rest

Mandating a 14-day resting period post-roast to allow completely degassed, stabilized extraction.

Singular Origins

Finca Lérida

Boquete, Panama • 1700m

A legendary estate bordering the La Amistad International Park. This washed Geisha lot exhibits the profound jasmine and bergamot aromatics that made the varietal famous, anchored by a delicate white peach sweetness.

  • Variety: Geisha
  • Process: Washed
  • Notes: Jasmine, Bergamot, White Peach

Uraga Tome

Guji, Ethiopia • 2100m

Grown at extreme altitudes under the shade of native forest canopy. This natural process lot was dried slowly over 28 days on raised beds, yielding an intensely syrupy body bursting with blueberry and violet.

  • Variety: Heirloom
  • Process: Natural (28 Days)
  • Notes: Blueberry, Violet, Cocoa Nib
