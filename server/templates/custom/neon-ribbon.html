The Algorithm

How the ribbon works

Four elegant steps that turn raw mouse coordinates into generative neon art.

01
Track Position

Every mousemove captures clientX/Y and diffs against the previous frame to compute velocity vectors.

02
Push Points

New point objects with position, velocity, size, and color are pushed onto a capped FIFO array of 120 entries.

03
Draw Curves

Triplets of consecutive points drive quadratic Bezier curves with opacity fading toward the tail end.

04
Composite & Glow

Lighter blend mode draws lines three times per frame, creating an additive glow where the ribbon overlaps itself.
