Our Story

Founded in 2010 by third-generation coffee roasters, Brew & Co began as a small family operation in the heart of the city. What started as a passion for perfecting the pour-over has grown into a beloved neighborhood café where locals gather to savor the art of coffee.

We source our beans directly from sustainable farms around the world, ensuring every cup supports ethical practices and exceptional flavor. Our baristas are trained to bring out the unique character of each bean, creating a truly memorable experience.
